What are Pierogi?

Pierogi are a traditional Polish dish of boiled or fried dough dumplings filled. Pierogi are often served with warm butter and carmelized onions. Pierogi can be hard to find in stores in the US, but they are becoming more popular and can even be found in Costco.

Types of Pierogi

Pierogi are served with potato and cheese, meat, apple, and blueberry stuffing.

When do I eat Pierogi

My family makes pierogi on all of the main holidays including Thanksgiving and Christmas. I have many fond memories of making pierogi on the kitchen table around the holidays. We also enjoy amazing Pierogi every time we visit Poland.
